Our Story

20 Years of Exceptional Care

For more than two decades, Golden Paws has been the trusted grooming destination for Greater Boston's most discerning pet families — where artistry meets genuine compassion.

We prioritize pet safety and well-being above all else. Whether it's our medicated healing shampoo for dogs with skin sensitivities, our gentle de-shedding treatments, or our specialized care for senior and anxious dogs — every decision we make is guided by what's best for your pet, not just what looks good.

Matted coats are always humanely shaved down rather than painfully brushed through, and every pet receives a 6-week grooming schedule recommendation to keep their coat healthy and comfortable year-round.
